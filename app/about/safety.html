CampusCache Safety Guidelines

CampusCache functions as a connection and communication service between potential student buyers and sellers of goods. Its purpose is to provide this service free of charge and free of hassle to students by eliminating middleman charges, sorting and comparing listings, and reducing risk for transactions. In order to utilize CampusCache to the fullest and safest potential, several key guidelines and suggestions should be followed while using CampusCache online and conducting transactions initiated by CampusCache.

Online Guidelines

Do not disclose personal information: While a name and contact information is required, other types of personal information such as address, birthdates, social security numbers, bank and credit information, passwords, and UNC personal identification numbers should never be shared.

Verify the identity of a buyer or seller: If buyer or seller of a product contacts you through email, verify that the email ends with some form of unc.edu to ensure affiliation with the university. Also, looking up a potential buyer or seller’s name on UNC’s online database can help verify the person’s identity. Cease contact with a potential buyer or seller if you do not feel comfortable communicating with him/her.


Transaction Guidelines

Meet in a public place: When conducting a transaction, never meet a potential seller or buyer in a private location. Meet in public locations such as the libraries, the Pit, the Student Union, or other UNC affiliated buildings with lots of students. Bring a friend if you do not feel comfortable making the transaction by yourself.

Only disclose specific details about a transaction, such as time and place, in private conversations (email, text, private messages, etc) and not on public forums or the comments section of this site.

Make and receive payments in cash: When a transaction has been negotiated, make purchases and only accept payments in cash. Checks, money orders, PayPal, and other types of payment mediums may bounce or be reverted. Using cash is the easiest way of ensuring that payment is properly handled.

Conduct transactions only with people affiliated with the university: CampusCache is designed specifically for trade between students and, potentially, faculty at UNC. Conduct transactions only with people affiliated with UNC to ensure safety.

Back out of a transaction at any time: Remember that you have the power to back out of a transaction at any time. Do not feel pressured to buy or sell anything if you have doubts about the transaction. You are not obligated to buy or sell anything even if you meet with a potential buyer or seller.

CampusCache reserves the right to remove, moderate, or change any listing or comment on campuscache.org for any reason. By reserving this right, CampusCache will attempt to maintain the best user experience for its users and a clean, non-offensive online exchange.

When using CampusCache, please adhere to the guidelines listed above. CampusCache is not responsible for any damage, loss of property, and/or endangerment of self or others that may occur during transactions. By following the guidelines listed above and practicing additional caution, you maximize your safety using CampusCache.
