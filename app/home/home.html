Our Mission:

TClassified provides students at UNC with an simple and effective means of buying and selling textbooks. Our application, built with the consumer in mind, is completely free to use, maximizes efficiency, and keeps the money of students in the pockets of students. Join us as we enter a new era where the backs of students are not weighed down by the crippling debt of education, where the buffalo roam, where the deer and antelope play, where seldom is heard a discouraging word, and where the skies are not cloudy all day.
