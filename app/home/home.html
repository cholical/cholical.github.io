Campus Cache at a Glance

Campus Cache provides students at UNC with a simple and effective platform for posting items for sale and simple jobs. Our application, built with the consumer in mind, is completely free to use, maximizes the efficiency of transactions, and keeps the money of students strictly in the pockets of students. Join us as we enter a new era where the backs of students are not bowed by the crippling debt of education, where businesses do not exist purely to make profit off of those who have no other choice, where the buffalo roam, where the deer and antelope play, where seldom is heard a discouraging word, and where the skies are not cloudy all day.

An interface designed with simplicity in mind

Listings are easy to find

We make it quick and easy to find the item you are looking for with a multitude of sorting and filtering options.

Dynamic Search

Search listings with any keywords pertaining to a listing, such as seller name, ISBN, Author, price, and so on.

View detailed information without leaving the page

Our fluid design allows you to click on any listing to see more about it without taking you to a new page, or reseting your filters and your search terms.

Create and edit listings: No account required!

When creating a listing, all that is required is a password. Use this password to easily edit your post later.

Full image gallery intergration

Upload up to four pictures to go along with your post, and viewers can see them in a convenient gallery in the info panel.
