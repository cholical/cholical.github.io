At a Glance

CampusCache provides students at UNC with a simple yet effective platform for listing items for sale and searching for opportunities. Our platform, built with the consumer in mind, maximizes the efficiency of transactions, keeps the money of students strictly in the pockets of students, and charges nothing to use. Join us as we enter a new era where the backs of students are not bowed by the crippling debt of education, where businesses are prevented from making profit off those who have no other choice, and where the skies are not cloudy all day.

An interface designed with simplicity in mind

Listings are easy to find

Find the exact textbook or item you need by using a variety of sorting and filtering options.

Fast and dynamic search bar

Search listings with any keywords such as seller name, ISBN, Author, price, and much more.

View detailed information without leaving the page

CampusCache's fluid design allows you to click on any listing to more detailed information without leaving the page.

Create and edit listings: No account required

Making an account is not required for creating listings. All that's needed is a password for easy editing of your posts later.

Image gallery integration

Upload up to four pictures to go along with your post. Viewers can see them in a convenient gallery in the info panel.

Comments system

Use the comments to negotiate pricing, clarify issues, or inquire more about a listing before finalizing a sale.
